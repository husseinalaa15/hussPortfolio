Overlooking Tiran Island, this luxe beachfront resort along the Red Sea is 7 km from the Coptic Church and 9 km from Sharm El Sheikh International Airport.

Featuring terraces or balconies, the bright rooms have free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minifridges. Suites add living areas. Upgraded rooms and suites (adults-only) are swim-up. Some suites, as well as villas with kitchens, offer private pools and/or sea views. Room service is available 24/7.

Breakfast is free. There are multiple bars, restaurants and outdoor pools, along with a kids’ club, a spa and a private area on the beach. Parking, scuba diving and an airport shuttle are available.

On a beach along the Red Sea, this plush all-inclusive hotel with a private jetty and a protected coral reef is 10 km from Sharm El Sheikh International Airport.

Posh rooms offer free Wi-Fi and minibars, plus flat-screens, tea and coffeemakers, and balconies or terraces. Some offer sea views and/or direct pool access. Suites add living rooms and/or whirlpool tubs. Luxe 2- or 3-bedroom villas include kitchenettes, private pools and butler service.

Meals and drinks are included. There are 7 restaurants and 9 bars. Other amenities consist of 8 pools (1 indoor), a gym and tennis, plus 2 spas (1 female-only) with saunas, hot tubs and hammams.

Offering views of the Nile river, this modern hotel in downtown Cairo is a 5-minute walk from the Egyptian Museum, and a 6-minute walk from Sadat metro station and the landmark Tahrir Square.

Unfussy rooms feature sitting areas, free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemakers. All have city, pool or river views. Suites add living rooms. Kids age 12 and under (1 per room) stay at no charge with a parent. Room service is available 24/7.

A contemporary restaurant serves international food. There's a cozy 24-hour lobby bar and another next to an outdoor pool. Other amenities include a fitness center and 2 saunas.

Set beachfront on Sahl Hasheesh Bay, this opulent resort is 26 km from Hurghada International Airport and 3 km from Hurghada's Old Town.

Choice rooms provide balconies, Wi-Fi, flat-screens and minibars, as well as tea and coffeemakers. Some offer bay views and access to a lounge; 1- or 2-bedroom suites add features such as private pools, while upgraded suites include dining areas, butler services and marble bathrooms with Jacuzzi tubs.

Breakfast and parking are free. The resort offers 7 restaurants, plus bars and live entertainment. There's a shopping arcade, sports facilities and a gym, as well as a dive center, an outdoor pool and a spa.

This sophisticated hotel in an affluent, downtown district is 2 km from both the Egyptian Museum and the Cairo Opera House.

Refined rooms have free Wi-Fi, safes and Nile River and city views. Upgraded rooms add balconies. Suites feature tea and coffeemakers, nonalcoholic minibars and living rooms. Upgraded suites offer kitchenettes; 1 has a kitchen and a whirlpool tub. Private butler service is available 24/7.

A breakfast buffet (fee) is served in a stylish Italian eatery. There's an upscale Ottoman restaurant, a cozy chocolate lounge, a chic piano bar, and a rooftop bar with shisha service. There's also a spa, a 24/7 gym and a rooftop pool.
